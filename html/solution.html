What you can do to help
the children in need

Rainwater Harvesting Jars

These jars are relativly cheap and it could be useful in placing near homes. They are collected through rainwater sources.

Rope Pumps

These pumps are sustainable and can be fixed into different locations cheap and easily using simple technology like recyclable bicycle wheels, scrap metal, and plastic.

Hand Pumps

Hand pumps are more convient as they are less contaminated by using pistons for suction lift, pump rods, and different mechanics depending on large or small pumps.

With the assistance of infrastructures such as rope pumps, gravity flow systems, and borehole drilling rigs, we can provide safe sanitized water to childrens and families.
