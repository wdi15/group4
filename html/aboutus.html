Who We Are

We are a group of 5 amateur coders who have over the course of 8 coding classes learned the basics of website development and have utlized those learned skills to create a website in the hopes of raising awareness of the importance of water as well as supporting WaterAid Canada in its efforts to provide clean water and safe sanitation to third world countries.
